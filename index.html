Balsamic Chicken

Preparation:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the balsamic vinegar with sugar and garlic. Place chicken breasts in the marinade, then, Whisk together to coat well on each side before placing them into the refrigerator for 10 minutes.

  2. In medium-high heat, warm some olive oil. Remove the chicken! Place these pieces into your heated pan, cooking them until they start to brown on each side but not too much, or else it will be raw inside - about 7 minutes per side depending upon how big those breasts are you're using (I recommend flipping mine once). Pour whatever remains of that liquid onto the top, so everything gets nicely coated together with an ultra-rich flavor.

Palestinian Sailor's Salad

Preparation:

Break apart the toasted bread. Chop chili peppers, removing some of the membranes and seeds. In a mortar and pestle, pound the peppers along with the salt until soft. Add the basil and muddle to extract flavor. Add the green onions and crush well, followed by the cucumbers. Finally, stir in the tomatoes and mash up. Mix the entire salad to incorporate all ingredients. In a separate bowl, whisk the tahini with the lemon juice until emulsified. Right before serving, mix the tahini sauce and bread pieces into the salad. Transfer to a serving dish and flatten the top. Drizzle the surface generously with a good quality olive oil. Garnish with pitted green olives, pickled peppers, and radish slices.
